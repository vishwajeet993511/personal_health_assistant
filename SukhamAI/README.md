# SUKHAM AI ✨

[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-Apache%202.0-blue.svg)](LICENSE)

**Your AI Partner in Holistic Wellbeing.**

Sukham AI is your intelligent, on-device health companion. Experience high-performance, private, and lightning-fast health insights directly on your hardware—fully offline.

## ✨ Core Features

* **Chat with AI**: Engage in fluid, multi-turn conversations with your personal wellness guide. Ask anything about symptoms, diet, or general wellness, anytime.

* **Wearables Data Analysis**: (Powered by Gemma 4 Multimodal) Understand your body better. Upload photos of your wearable data or health reports to get detailed, AI-powered insights and descriptions.

* **Live Exercise**: Join expert-led sessions in real time and stay active.

* **Yoga Tips**: Get daily guidance for your body, mind, and soul with personalized yoga recommendations.

* **Personal Files Analysis**: Securely upload your health reports and records to get AI-driven analysis and insights—all processed locally on your device.

* ** Food and Daily Exercises** Recommendation

* **Share My Progress**: Celebrate milestones and inspire others. Let your loved ones be part of your fitness journey by sharing your progress securely.

* **100% On-Device Privacy**: Your health is personal. All model inferences happen directly on your device. No internet is required, ensuring total privacy for your sensitive data.

## 🛠️ Technology Highlights

*   **Gemma 4 (2B Multimodal):** Cutting-edge on-device multimodal LLM for image and text understanding.
*   **Google AI Edge:** Core APIs and tools for high-efficiency on-device ML.
*   **LiteRT:** Lightweight runtime for optimized model execution on mobile.
